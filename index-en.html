My experience in LU

The Bachelor of Computer Science study program at the University of Latvia offers a broad and versatile insight into the fundamentals and practice of computer science. From a student's perspective, this course is challenging, but also very valuable - it develops analytical thinking, programming skills and an understanding of algorithms, data structures, artificial intelligence, network security and other technical topics. The studies require perseverance and an interest in technology, but they also provide an opportunity to work on real projects, collaborate with other students and gain a solid foundation in the IT industry.
