Studying computer science at the University of Latvia means gaining both theoretical knowledge and practical skills in the field of modern technologies. During their studies, students learn programming, algorithms, data structures, artificial intelligence and other topics that help prepare them for work in the IT industry. Lectures and practical classes are well organized, and the teachers try to help each student understand complex issues and develop analytical thinking.

One of the special events that unites computer science students is “Aristotelis” - a musical pre-studies event that always takes place on the last Sunday before the start of the fall semester. It features several musicians and bands, and it is a great opportunity to meet fellow students, relax, and gain energy before the intensive academic year. Students also actively participate in “Sejena” - a large hike with various activities that promote friendship and team spirit among students.

Studies in computer science at the University of Latvia also provide great career opportunities, as graduates are in high demand both in the local and international labor market. After the bachelor's course, you can continue your studies in a master's degree, specializing in even more specific areas, such as data analysis or cybersecurity. The skills acquired in the program help you successfully start in the IT industry and develop a career in the field of technology.
